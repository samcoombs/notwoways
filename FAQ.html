Faq

What time will Subsolar be released?

18:00 GMT (UK Time).

Do you need a password to access the product page and buy?

No, the stock will be activated at 18:00 GMT. Please refresh the page at the time of release and add the product to your basket.

Will anything else be dropping on the 8th of March 2021?

Yes, a very limited number of notwoways socks will also be released.

How many pairs can I buy and will you do anything to stop bots?

It’s important to us to make each drop as fair as possible, therefore a limit of 1 pair per customer will be in place. We have added increased levels of bot protection.

How much do the shoes cost?

The shoes will retail at £100.

Are the shoes waterproof?

Subsolar uses a water-tight backing that will keep your feet dry. But to keep the suede uppers soft, a protective layer is recommended.

What is the size range?

UK3 to UK13.

How do the shoes fit?

The shoes fit true-to-size.

I’m between sizes, is it best to size up or down?

- It really depends on the width of your feet and how you like your shoes to fit. See below for some examples:

- If you have an average width and like a secure/tight fit, go down (eg. UK8.5, go for a UK8)

- If you have an average width and like a loose/relaxed fit, go up (eg. UK8.5, go for a UK9)

- If you have wide feet, go up (eg. UK8.5, go for a UK9)


Please check the size chart on the product page for further information. Or contact us on IG if you are really unsure.

Is the fitting the same as the previous (moonless) drop?

Yes, the subsolar fits very similar to moonless. However, we added extra support around the achilles-tendon and placed an EVA footbed beneath the insole. Based on the feedback thus far, these are the most comfortable pair yet.

Is the shipping cost included in the price?

No, shipping costs will be calculated at checkout.

Do you ship worldwide and which couriers do you use?

Yes we ship worldwide! Due to some delays in the past, we have now upgraded our shipping couriers:

UK - Parcelforce

Europe - Parcelforce or DPD (Dependent on location)

USA - FedEx

Rest of World - FedEx


Please note: Shipping cost varies with each country, you will be able to see this during checkout. The UK has recently left the EU, this may affect the final shipping price.

Will Brexit affect my order?

Now that Britain has left the EU, orders placed from countries that are part of the EU will be required to pay duties and tax for all imported goods. The amount differs from country to country, it is best to check your government website for more specific details. Please note, we cannot cover these costs for you.

How will the shoes be shipped?

We will ship the shoes double-boxed to make sure they arrive safely.

Will there be a way to pre-input my address information before the drop?

Yes, if you make an account you can save your shipping details prior to the release. We highly recommend doing this to have the best chance of grabbing a pair.

Will you offer returns if the shoes don’t fit or are damaged?

Yes, given they are new and in unworn condition and all tags / stickers are attached, you have 14 days (from the date it is delivered) to return the shoes to us. Please see our return policy for more details.

Will you have any restocks in the future?

No, once the colourway is sold-out they are gone for good.

Will you have more shoes coming out in the future?

Yes, have some big plans this year. Check our instagram for updates and vote for your favourite colourways! But for now, subsolar..
